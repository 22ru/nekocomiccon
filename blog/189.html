January 11, 2024

I must share with you the worst experience of my life today.

I was preparing a Very Delicious Morning Latte this morning. I finished the coffee parts as well as Ryan's milk. I delivered his latte to him.

I got my milk ready for the big angry swirl, and saw something move to my side. I looked over and found a spider running laps around the rim of my filled coffee cup.

As any sane person would do, I started screaming!!! Ryan did not come quick enough to help me with any ideas with what to do about this enthused and athletic spider.

And just like that, he jumped into my coffee.

Ryan pulled the spider out and offered to trade me for his partially sipped latte. Thank you Ryan...

He has informed me he is able to shoot webs from his hands now.
