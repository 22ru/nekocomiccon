I was wanting to try making monpe so I was googling around and I found this pattern made of boxes and triangles. I divided the measurements by 3 and started cutting.

My first mistake was to NOT look at the diagram while assembling them. I paid for that mistake in seam ripping. There's an unavoidable 3 seam intersection that I found difficult to sew, but the rest of it was fairly simple (after looking at the instructions).

I'm having Cheby model it, but I think this color combo would look much better on Verona. I'll have to make these pieces again in black or something.
