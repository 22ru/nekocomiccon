My Opinion is Law

I said I wouldn't make a collection page for my CDs but I (or Ryan) do own all of these. I won't rate a greatest hits album here. That's cheating.

a-ha - Hunting High And Low ★★★★★

I probably spent days listening to this album while sewing doll clothes as a teen.

Ace of Base - The Sign ★★★★☆

I listen to it on repeat.

Alphaville - Forever Young ★★★★☆

I expected all of the album to sound like Forever Young, but that's the slowest one on the album. Allegedly, there's a faster version of Forever Young out there. Please email me if you have it. Favorite song: Fallen Angel

Bjork - Post ★★★★☆

Delete "It's Oh So Quiet" before listening and you have a great album!

Creed - Weathered ★★★★☆

We had a ripped copy of this CD when I was a kid and I spent a lot of time making fun of it but it's really fun to sing.

Daft Punk - Random Access Memories ★★★☆☆

There's a few pretty good ones! I really like Fragments of Time, Doin' It Right, Instant Crush. Some others are easily skippable.

David Bowie - The Rise And Fall Of Ziggy Stardust And The Spiders From Mars ★★★★★

It's the best Bowie album of all time. You can't change my mind.

Depeche Mode - Violator ★★★★★

Favorite song: Enjoy the Silence

Edie Brickell & The New Bohemians - Shooting Rubberbands at the Moon ★☆☆☆☆

What I Am is good but the rest of the album means nothing to me.

Eurythmics - Revenge ★★☆☆☆

I can't remember any of this album despite having listened to it a few times.

Howard Jones - The 12" Album ★★★★☆

6 Very Good Songs

Huey Lewis And The News - Sports ★★★★☆

I don't think there's anything I can complain about. I have the expanded one with Live versions, which I dislike.

The Human League - Dare ★★★☆☆

It's decent!

Incubus - Make Yourself ★★★★☆

Justin Bieber - My World 2.0 ★★★★★

It's so cute! I love it. It's fun to sing and poppy! What more could you need in life?! HE'S SO CUTE

Justin Bieber - Believe ★★★★☆

Another great J Biebz album. Maria was a bad take. Favorite song: Thought of You

Justin Bieber - Purpose ★★★☆☆

Still a good album, but on the decline. Favorite song: Been You (a Target exclusive?!)

Justin Bieber - Changes ★☆☆☆☆

I'm not sure I liked any of the songs on this album. I was really excited for it too. I probably won't buy the next one.

Justin Timberlake - Justified ★★★☆☆

It's catchy and good but is he allowed to sing that??? The lyrics are pretty spicy.

Justin Timberlake - The 20/20 Experience ★★★☆☆

I spent many years never being able to get past Mirrors, but I eventually did and the rest of the album was good too. Favorite song: Mirrors

Ke$ha - Warrior ★★☆☆☆

Fun beats but holy cow the whole album is a love letter to degeneracy.

Milli Vanilli - Girl You Know It's True ★★☆☆☆

Nothing notable.

Muse - Showbiz ★★☆☆☆

Unexciting album. Whiny voice and no bangers.

Oingo Boingo - Only a Lad ★★☆☆☆

I think this one is just a little too silly for me.

Savage Garden - Savage Garden ★★★★★

I kept calling it the Jojo OST but I actually listened to it and then listened to it like 20 more times. I like in Santa Monica where he sings that you can be anyone you want on the phone. Same.

Savage Garden - Affirmation ★★☆☆☆

Some good songs, some bad songs. There's a few that will make me mad to listen to but then there's The Best Thing, which I think is one of their top bangers. He was definitely divorce bitter at the time of writing this album.

Seal - Seal (1991) ★★★★☆

It's a really good one! I had a mix CD with Crazy on it back in the day and it's good to hear it again. I didn't originally like Killer, but I'm into it now.

Seal - Seal (1994) ★★★★☆

Jesus Christ why did you name two albums the same thing. Whatever. They're both great. Favorite song: Kiss from a Rose (duh)

Siouxsie and the Banshees - Peepshow ★★☆☆☆

It's just so inoffensive. I don't even care.

Staind - Dysfunction ★★★☆☆

Staind - Break The Cycle ★★★★☆

Many fun songs to sing!

Staind - 12 Shades of Grey ★★★★☆
