franchan / maximumfaggotry / maxfag

In 2013, I decided I wanted to make an imageboard! I found tinyboard, installed it on a free webhost, and made a few boards including /jb/ - Justin Bieber, /doll/ - BJD, /qt/ - cute boy. I managed to get a few friends to post on it with me, but it was really empty.

Also in 2013, the seagulls had complained enough that moot had come to the board to take suggestions for improvement to moderation. Janitors were put in place and any mention of drama involving lolcows was removed.

FunkBrothers suggested to me a few times that I should add a pixyteri board on my imageboard. I've always been one for shitposting, but not really one for whatever this is. I declined a few times, but eventually caved. I added /pt/ - PXEteri which he linked on /cgl/ shortly after.

It was quiet at first, but suddenly there were tons of people visiting that board! The free host I was using did not allow NSFW content and couldn't handle the traffic, so I had to switch to paid hosting, despite not having any form of income and angry anons not willing to donate. I moved only the drama boards to paid hosting since the boards made for me were doing just fine.

At some point, some users tried to doxx me. They got it wrong, but I gave up on them. There's no point in adding misery to your life for people you don't know and hate you. I didn't renew hosting and abandoned the board. My other boards stayed up for a while after, but were eventually removed by the free host for lack of traffic.
