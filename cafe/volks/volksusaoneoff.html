VolksUSA One-Offs

Once a year and for special events, VolksUSA offers one-off models. You can still see many of the pages after the web store and management change in 2014.

Lottery entries are accepted from the 1st to 10th of the month and winners announced.... sometime...

Limited and FCS models are offered. Pricing is a mystery, but larger dolls cost more. Volks designed outfits are generally used and not dealer made outfits.
