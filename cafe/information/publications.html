Japan Ai: A Tall Girl's Adventures in Japan ★

An illustrated story about Aimee Major's trip to Glorious Nippon. Select pages are in color. Very cute and enjoyable, but not very long. The final chapter covers her visit to Tenshi no Sato in Kyoto. She has a map of TnS, describes her FCS order, her omukae ceremony, and her dinner with the Shigeta family.
