Kinoko Juice Kiki Munyukuchi

August 11, 2022

I'm a little shocked at how small this box is!

And the box inside was even smaller!

Ryan asked my kikipop wasn't attending the opening! So I brought her over :3

I'm pretty sure every KJ doll arrives with their headcap floating. No damage, but I wish people would tape them down with masking tape or just wrap them separately.

She is WHITE. Paper white! She makes Hina look tan. It's crazy! She has little custom eyes that are light blue and have a little ghost in each instead of a pupil.

My kiki came with a kiki keychain, a laminated doll data card, 4 postcard sized prints, and a letter I cannot pretend to read. The prints are all double sided! Shizuku a cute!

I'm not so sure about this pleather sueding disc. This doll is just over 2 years old and the pleather is leaving brown residue all over the top of her neck. I already took it off. Maybe I'll find some real leather to replace it with.
