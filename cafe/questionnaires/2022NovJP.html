ドールオーナーがいいねの数だけ答える

"Answer as many times as the doll owner likes"

Completed November 27, 2022

This questionnaire first appeared on Twitter on November 14, 2022. All translations are by google and edited by me for clarity. I do take constructive criticism. The blank version is here for you to fill out yourself!

  1. うちの子は全部で何人？ How many dolls do you have in total?

    2. 11 BJD, 2 not-quite-BJD, 2 Licca, 5 other. Also a pile of barbies and other dolls in my mom's basement. Let's go with 20?

  2. 好きなスキンカラーは？ What is your favorite resin tone?

    3. I can't choose between NS and WS. WS has a more radiant feeling, but both are good. Paper white isn't what I mean by white either. Praline does not count!

    Now I have no idea what color I'll decide on for my 58chan...

  3. 3.うちの子の設定はある？ Do you have backstories for your dolls?

    4. All of the 1/3rd resin dolls have some sort of story to them. No one smaller does. The first three boys had profiles and characters written from when I was a teen. I no longer write stories or comics about them, but their relationships kind of developed in my head anyway. I can't say I won't draw them though.

    I moved away from thinking of stories for them for a long time, but it is really fun to imagine Liam leaving Hina at the altar to run off with his ex bf Verona. (Not yet canon)

  4. ドールの名前の決め方は？ How do you decide on your dolls' names?

    5. They're mostly from songs, locations, or just cute!

  5. いつかお迎えしたい子は？ Which doll would you like to buy someday?

    6. 58chan. Codi, do your best! Cheby needs a girl!

  6. ドールとお出かけする？ Do you go out with dolls?

    7. Not unless it's to take photos. I'd also take them out for a meetup!

  7. リアクロ派、ドレス派？ Do you prefer casual clothes or dress clothes?

    8. Dress clothes but I'm not skilled enough to make them yet.

  8. ドールに触れる頻度は？ How often do you play with your dolls?

    9. I take the ones in my chest out for smoochies at least a couple times a week.

    Others may not see the light of day for months.

  9. よく利用するショップは？ What shops do you frequent?

    10. None...

  10. 撮影で気をつけていることは？ What do you pay attention to when shooting?

    11. Getting at least one eye in focus. I want to improve at creating natural and more interesting poses though.

  11. あると便利なドール用品は？ Do you own any items that make doll ownership or photography more convienent?

    12. Crochet hooks, a piece of wire bent in half, a hairbrush and comb (not used on human hair), and toothbrush.

  12. お着替えさせる頻度は？ How often do you change their clothes?

    13. Depends on what they're wearing. It takes at least half an hour to get Hina in her kimono, so if I put that on, it's staying on for a few months.

    The boys don't really have much to switch out into but sometimes I'll swap pieces between them.

    I haven't played much with my minis so I don't think they've been changed in a year.

  13. どんな写真の雰囲気が好き？ What kind of atmosphere do you like in photos?

    14. I really love to look at the landscape + doll photography. I do none of this myself.

  14. ドールと行ってみたい場所は？ Where would you like to take your dolls?

    15. I want to take Hina to Tenshi no Sato for an omukae ceremony ;________;

  15. 一番お金を掛けているのは？ What do you spend the most money on?

    16. The doll itself. I'm too stingy to spend money on anything else. It's a curse.

  16. 今一番欲しいものは？ What do you want most right now?

    17. New wigs, shoes, and eyes for my current dolls. I know where I would get wigs but I'm stumped on everything else.

  17. 好きなポージングは？ What is your favorite pose?

    18. This one. I'm gonna need another girl. Wait, can I get my boys to do this??

  18. ドール服収納どうしてる？ How do you store doll clothes?

    19. In the cardboard photo boxes they sell at Joanns, Hobby Lobby, etc. I also store wigs, shoes, and other accessories in them. Mine are a little full now...

  19. 推しメーカー、推しヘッドは？ What company and face sculpt do you recommend?

    20. Wow!! You want a Lieselotte?! Good choice!

  20. 一年で最高何人お迎えした？ How many dolls have you received in one year?

    21. I received 3 and a head in 2009. I received 3 in 2010. Yikes...

  21. 好きなスタジオは? What is your favorite studio?

    22. I haven't taken my dolls to a photo studio, so I will say the cherry trees nearby when they are in bloom.

  22. 今後撮ってみたい写真は？ What kind of photos would you like to take in the future?

    23. Ones that tell a little story between the dolls in the photo.

  23. うちの子の中で最推しがいる？ Do you have a favorite among your dolls?

    24. Hina. hina hina hina.

  24. どんなシチュの撮影が好き？ What kind of situations do you like to convey in your photos?

    25. Goofy ones like Praline in dumb situations.

    I secretly love shipping my dolls... Shh.... Those are a secret and you might have to go searching for those.

  25. 一目惚れでお迎えしたことは？ Have you ever bought a doll on first sight?

    26. I've never been to a physical BJD store so I can't say I've picked up a coordinate model or in stock doll. Sochi would probably be the closest. I saw my friend had her listed on DoA and asked her to bring her. I paid for her after assembling her.

  26. 行く又は行ってみたいイベントは？ What events do you want to go to?

    27. Any Japanese event! Dolpa would be great, but the non-Volks events would also be fun. The dealers halls in Japan are much more creative than the ones in the US.

  27. 今後挑戦してみたいことは？ What do you want to try in the future?

    28. A DearSD. I can't tell if I would love her like my 1/3rd dolls though. I might enjoy my 1/3rd scale dolls because they look closer to adults and I can make my own little stories about them together. DSD are beyond cute but perhaps too young for me to enjoy thinking of a backstory for.

  28. お迎え待ちの子はいる？ Do you currently have any dolls on order?

    29. I am waiting for nothing from anyone.

  29. お洋服は買う派？作る派？ Do you buy clothes or make them?

    30. I mostly make them! I have some old pieces from 10 years ago that were purchased but I do enjoy making clothes for them myself.

  30. あったらいいなと思うドール用品は？ What doll items would you like to own?

    31. Hands! More hands! I would kill to have a set of hands for each of my boys. The iplehouse oYID static sausage hands make shooting so much less fun. I wish I had at least fists for them.

  31. ウィッグとアイは固定派？非固定派？ Do your dolls have a permanent pair of eyes or wig?

    32. Hina's heterochromia was temporary at first but now she's stuck with them. I can't imagine her without them anymore. Liam really needs a new wig, but I don't really share his wig with anyone else. Everything else can be swapped at any time.

  32. 撮影使用機材は？ What equipment did you use?

    33. Sony a7II, Canon FD 50mm f/1.4, 2 selfie ringlights, LED string lights, gameboy camera, a couple lens filters. Some doll stands too!

  33. フォロワーさんとしてみたいことは？ How do you like to interact as a follower?

    34. I hate being a follower and would rather be a friend if I am speaking to you. My interactions will be limited to saving your photo or linking your site if I am not talking to you.

  34. うちの子はオーナーを認識してる？ Does your doll recognize you as its owner?

    35. Hina is my boss and I can tell when she refuses to cooperate during photoshoots.

  35. 地雷はある？ Do you have any touchy subjects?

    36. Doll shoes? My hot takes on many doll companies? Choo? Do not talk to me about Danny Choo.

  36. リアルな友達にドールオーナーはいる？ Are there any doll owners you spend time with in real life?

    37. Not really anymore. My best friend has dolls and I see her maybe once a year, but we don't bring dolls to each other.

  37. 撮影は室内、スタジオ、野外派？ Do you shoot indoors, in the studio, or outdoors?

    38. Indoors and outdoors. I don't even think I could afford a studio if I did have one accessible to me.

  38. フォロワーさんへ一言 Write a message to your followers

    39. Hello? Is anyone other than hors reading this??

  39. 初心者ドールオーナーさんへ一言 Write a message to new doll owners

    40. Don't settle! Starter doll is a meme! Do your research!

  40. ここでしか言えないこと Write something you can only say here

    41. thanks for coming to my ted talk.
