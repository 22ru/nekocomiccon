welcome to my gear thread

This is my secondary hobby! My favorite subjects are dolls, Ryan, flowers, and baby animals, and also grown animals. I'm always trying to find a way to make my camera fit safely in my purse so I go through thrift store camera bags like crazy trying to find the smallest padded bag I can fit my camera in.

I don't try to remove the exif data on most photos if you're curious about what I used. Sometimes mogrify removes it without asking. You're not likely to learn much since I use vintage lenses 99% of the time.

Lenses

I am using the basic Fotodiox FD-NEX adapter and K/AR-NEX adapter. I prefer prime to zoom but who am I to say no to a deal?

Maker Mount Focal length Aperture Filter size
Sony FE 50mm f/1.8 49mm
The first lens I got with my camera body. There's not a lot of times that I use autofocus, but if I need it, I'll bring this lens. I'm very thankful I did not buy a zoom kit lens.
Canon FD 50mm f/1.4 52mm
This is my favorite lens and I will not apologize for abusing f/1.4 Most of my photos are shot with this lens.
Canon FD 50mm f/1.8 52mm
I have no reason to use this lens and I can't recall if I have at all.
Canon FL 35mm f/2.5 58mm
The aperture ring design is unlike any other lens I have. It's not super sharp on the edges of the shot. This works with the FD adapter if the aperture lever pin is removed from the adapter. It's a pain to unscrew and rescrew so I don't like swapping to it.
CIMKO FD 28-105mm f/3.5-4.9 67mm
Full title: CIMKO MT-X 28-105mm f/3.5-4.9 Macro. I haven't found much info on this lens or even the brand. The minimum focus distance is 2.5m, which is way too large for me. It doesn't seem to focus in macro mode. I have no idea if it can't focus in macro mode, if it's broken, or if I just can't figure it out. help
Canon FD 35-70mm f/3.5-4.5 52mm
I haven't used this one much because I'd rather use a prime in this range.
Canon FD 75-200mm f/4.5 52mm
If you carry this lens, people will approach you as if you are a photographer. The minimum focal distance is larger on this one at 1.8m, but I have a set of 52mm close up filters so I can still use for close ups. There's a ring to change to macro mode, but it only can be turned at a focal length of 75mm. To focus in macro mode, you turn the macro ring instead of the usual focus ring.
Konica AR 57mm f/1.4 55mm
Still testing!
Seikanon MC FD 28mm f/2.8 55mm
I have no idea how to stop down this one. Please, mysterious camera fairy, tell me the secret!! It's probably just broken.
