TH05 Mystic Square

Worn at: Emerald City Comicon 2014, Sakuracon 2014


I figured, "Oh a circle skirt plus the blouse I have and I already have the wig, this should be easy and not take long at all!". I was so wrong.

I decided to make her design more detailed and changed some things. Each layer of ruffles is over 6.5 yards long. There are 2 layers of ruffles on the overskirt and 1 layer on the underskirt. I bought the blouse and petticoat off Taobao for lolita few years back.

All in all, the only things she shares with bigger Alice is the petticoat, grimoire and wig.
