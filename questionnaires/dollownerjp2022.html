ドールオーナーがいいねの数だけ答える

This questionnaire first appeared on Twitter on November 14, 2022.

  1. うちの子は全部で何人？ How many children do you have in total?
  2. 好きなスキンカラーは？ What is your favorite skin color?
  3. 3.うちの子の設定はある？ Do you have a setting for my child?
  4. ドールの名前の決め方は？ How do you decide on the name of the doll?
  5. いつかお迎えしたい子は？ Which child would you like to adopt someday?
  6. ドールとお出かけする？ Do you go out with dolls?
  7. リアクロ派、ドレス派？ Do you prefer rear-cropping or dresses?
  8. ドールに触れる頻度は？ How often do you touch the doll?
  9. よく利用するショップは？ What shops do you often visit?
  10. 撮影で気をつけていることは？ What do you pay attention to when shooting?
  11. あると便利なドール用品は？ Do you have any doll accessories that are convenient to have?
  12. お着替えさせる頻度は？ How often do you change clothes?
  13. どんな写真の雰囲気が好き？ What kind of photo atmosphere do you like?
  14. ドールと行ってみたい場所は？ Where would you like to go with Doll?
  15. 一番お金を掛けているのは？ What do you spend the most money on?
  16. 今一番欲しいものは？ What do you want most right now?
  17. 好きなポージングは？ What is your favorite pose?
