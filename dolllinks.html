links (for doll lovers only)

a lot of these people fit under multiple labels! i'm sorry if i listed you here and you're not where you wanted to be!

Blogs

  1. elphsnt's My Dolls and Bears - Japan based collector with many beautiful Volks girls whose names all begin with the letter A. Blogs in English and Japanese.
  2. Amaranth's Castaways - Blog of a former recast supporter with lots of articles on identifying recasts as well as issues with confidence, selling, and consumerism.
  3. BJD Life - Asenva's blog. She makes a lot of Youtube videos. She's always upbeat and mature and I can really appreciate that!
  4. Val's World - Oldschool website of a doll collector who passed away in 2020. Not actually a blog but definitely worth checking out.

Sculptors

  1. Expensive Garbage - The site of a Chinese doll sculptor. Tells the story of her life through 6 dolls she has sculpted.

Customizers

  1. Pleiades Room - American faceup artist and photographer.
  2. Jadepixel Doll Lab - American faceup artist, seamstress, photographer.

Photographers

i refuse to accept instagram as a platform for sharing photographs.

  1. AZURE Toy-box - Photo blog of an amazing DD photographer.
  2. Orchid dolls - Blog of a Spanish DD collector. She posts photography tips in English.
  3. Zodake - Thai DD photographer. Owns a doll cafe. Photoshop wizard and Miku fanatic. Before you ask, all the joints are photoshopped out.
  4. nono nogisaka - Japanese DD photographer. Lots of landscapes with dolls in TOAClaris clothes.
  5. nisshi - Japanese DD photographer. More pretty girls in landscapes.
  6. kotonokazu - Japanese doll photographer and doll tailor. Beautiful Volks boys! Fujos should check this guy out.
  7. Hiritai - Blog of an Australian girl who had some beautiful boy dolls. The inspiration of many young doll fujoshis.
  8. Johnny Director - Japanese cowboy doll photographer.
  9. Tom Beach - Depression era doll photographer. This dude follows his dreams.
  10. bottlefairy - Japanese DD photographer. I think this dude owns a lot of Sabers. Like at least 5. Some photos are NSFW.
  11. manjuu NEW - Japanese DD photographer. Dynamic poses sometimes with weapons. Some photos are NSFW.

YouTube Channels

  1. Inari Passenger - Russian girl living in Tokyo. She has some very detailed box openings of Japanese dolls including Volks and Azone.
  2. Rokuドールch - Sewing and doll crafting videos in Japanese.
  3. AsenvaBJD - Once famous for JunkySpot Obitsu reviews. Still making videos about having fun with her dolls. She's good at not getting into drama.
  4. Lomi's Playground - This girl has better sewing techniques than I do. She has patterns available for most if not all of her sewing tutorials. She seems to have a lot of fun customizing her dolls!
  5. Mina & Tachibana - I don't really enjoy listening to Chinese, but if you want to watch 2 girls open some stupidly expensive dolls, check these girls out.
