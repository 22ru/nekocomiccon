links (for doll lovers only)

photos and blogs

  1. AZURE Toy-box - Photo blog of an amazing DD photographer.
  2. Johnny Director - Japanese cowboy doll photographer.
  3. kotonokazu - Japanese doll photographer and doll tailor. Beautiful Volks boys! Fujos should check this guy out.
  4. Zodake - Thai DD photographer. Owns a doll cafe. Photoshop wizard and Miku fanatic.
  5. Expensive Garbage - The site of a Chinese doll sculptor. Tells the story of her life through 6 dolls she has sculpted.
  6. Hiritai - Blog of an Australian girl who had some beautiful boy dolls. The inspiration of many young doll fujoshis.
  7. Orchid dolls - Blog of a Spanish DD collector. She posts photography tips in English.
  8. elphsnt's My Dolls and Bears - Japan based collector with many beautiful Volks girls whose names all begin with the letter A. Blogs in English and Japanese.
  9. Pleiades Room - American faceup artist and photographer.
  10. Jadepixel Doll Lab - American faceup artist, seamstress, photographer.
  11. Amaranth's Castaways - Blog of a former recast supporter with lots of articles on identifying recasts as well as issues with confidence, selling, and consumerism.
  12. BJD Life - Asenva's blog! She makes a lot of Youtube videos. She's always upbeat and mature and I can really appreciate that!
  13. Val's World - Oldschool website of a doll collector who passed away in 2020.
  14. Tom Beach - Depression era doll photographer. This dude follows his dreams.
