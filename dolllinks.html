links (for doll lovers only)

Unofficial BJD Reference Sites in English

  • Tenshi no Kichi - Volks. Covers a lot of topics! Events, pages for each doll sculpt with info on one-off models released. A bit of info on FCS, not really any photos though. Good place to waste time looking at the limited doll you want!
  • ningyoBINGO's Neo Angel Den - Volks. English FCS guide. Based off of Angel Den with updated information. Lots of photos so you can dream about going to Japan and ordering an FCS doll.
  • Angel Den - Volks. The original FCS guide in English. Also has an area for every standard and limited model doll. Stopped being updated in Sep 2012. No photos anymore. Thanks for your hard work seraphim! If you need an icon for your DoA signature, she's got you covered.
  • Cerberus Project BJD Database - CP. Database of dolls created by early BJD sculptors that would eventually become Fairyland. I wish you luck in creating your own personal El army.

Blogs

  1. elphsnt's My Dolls and Bears - English and Japanese. Japan based collector with many beautiful Volks girls whose names all begin with the letter A.
  2. Amaranth's Castaways - Blog of a former recast supporter with lots of articles on identifying recasts as well as issues with confidence, selling, and consumerism.
  3. BJD Life - Asenva's blog. She makes a lot of Youtube videos. She's always upbeat and mature and I can really appreciate that!
  4. たいたんnoページ - Japanese. I could but this under photographers, but it's really an event report blog. Hasn't been updated since 2016.
  5. linuxhacker.ru (archived) - English and Russian. Disappeared rather recently, but last updated in 2018. Includes dollpa photos! Here is their flickr
  6. Pepito - Japanese. Lots of nendo and Volks doll blogging. Very cute! Has a few beautiful one-off girls.

Sculptors

  1. Expensive Garbage - The site of a Chinese doll sculptor. Tells the story of her life through 6 dolls she has sculpted.
  2. Nunodoll - Tutorial on how to make a cute fabric doll! I used this to make Ryan a Takane doll a few years ago.
  3. Vivcore's Dolly Daydream - Step by step how a woman created her first BJD. Perhaps not the most impressive doll, but the process is fun! Her name sounds familiar...
  4. Denes - Korean sculptor that has sculpted for many Korean doll companies.
